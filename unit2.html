Origin of The Universe

      Scientists belive that the universe originated as one single point and expanded rapidly. This theory is called the big bang. They believe the universe is still expanding to this day. The proof they have to prove that it is still expanding is called redshift. Redshift is when the apparent wavelength of an object lengthens and moves to the infrared side of the electromagnetic spectrum. Another peice of evidence supporting the big bang theory is cosmic microwave background (CMB) radiation, belived to be left over from the big bang.
